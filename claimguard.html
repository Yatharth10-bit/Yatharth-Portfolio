ClaimGuard

An AI compliance monitor that helps food, supplement, and wellness brands catch risky product claims before they become expensive regulatory problems.

RoleDesign + Development
TypeSaaS Product
StackReact · Next.js · AI
Year2025

The problem

Small food and wellness brands often publish product claims without a compliance system in place. FDA and FTC rules are complex, updates are constant, and one risky line of copy can turn into a costly fix later.

The approach

ClaimGuard combines regulatory monitoring with claim-level risk review. Instead of a static checklist, it watches for updates, flags high-risk language, and suggests safer rewrites — so teams can act before launch.

What I built

  • Regulatory update feed tailored to brand category
  • AI-assisted claim risk scoring and flagging
  • Safer rewrite suggestions for marketing copy
  • Product-level compliance dashboard
  • Onboarding flow to personalize risk profiles

Outcome

A working SaaS prototype that turns compliance from a reactive scramble into a proactive workflow — especially for small brands that cannot afford dedicated legal teams.
