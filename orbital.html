ORBITAL

A real-time 3D satellite tracker that visualizes more than 20,000 active objects orbiting Earth — built to make orbital data feel immersive and explorable.

RoleDesign + Development
TypeInteractive Web
StackThree.js · WebGL
Year2024 — 2025

The idea

Space data is usually trapped in technical interfaces. I wanted to build something that feels alive — a globe you can navigate, satellites you can follow, and orbital motion you can actually see updating in real time.

The experience

ORBITAL places the user inside a 3D scene with Earth at the center and thousands of objects moving along their paths. You can explore by type, focus on individual satellites, and watch the system evolve as you interact.

What I built

  • 3D globe with real-time orbital visualization
  • 20,000+ tracked objects with live path updates
  • Filtering by satellite type and category
  • Individual satellite follow mode
  • Performance-tuned rendering for smooth navigation

Outcome

An interactive web experience that merges engineering and design — turning raw orbital data into something visual, intuitive, and genuinely fun to explore.
